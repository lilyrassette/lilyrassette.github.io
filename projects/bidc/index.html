Printing & Prototyping Supervisor

These are projects I worked on as a Printing & Prototyping Supervisor at the Bechtel Innovation Design Center (BIDC) at Purdue University. My role involves overseeing fabrication tools, assisting students, and performing maintenance and lab improvement tasks.

Hands-on Fabrication & Design Leadership

In my time at the BIDC, I’ve worked with a wide range of fabrication technologies — from additive manufacturing and laser cutting to metal casting and composite layups. I love creating, troubleshooting, and helping others bring ideas to life.

3D Printed Wax Casted Ring

I taught myself Blender to design a personalized ring, 3D printed it on a Formlabs Form 3B in wax resin, and used the print as a mold for casting the final piece in Nordic gold. This project taught me about precision modeling, additive design constraints, and metal casting processes.

  • Skills: Lost Wax Casting, 3D Modeling, Additive Manufacturing
  • Tools: Blender, Fusion360, Formlabs PreForm

Mentoring Students & Researchers

I’ve mentored numerous students and research teams in using 3D printers, laser cutters, and metal fabrication tools safely and effectively. From troubleshooting prints to guiding mechanical design choices, I’ve learned to communicate technical processes clearly and adapt quickly to diverse projects.

  • Example Projects: Roll cage parts for Formula SAE, carbon fiber adapters, foldable 3D printed stool, biomedical wearables.
  • Focus Areas: Design for Manufacturability, Team Training, Problem Solving

Maintaining & Improving the Lab

As a supervisor, I not only support students but also keep the lab running smoothly — performing maintenance on printers, CNCs, and lasers, and leading upgrades that make the space safer and more efficient.

Machine Maintenance & Lab Improvements

I’ve overhauled printer calibration routines, replaced parts on CNC gantries, and implemented a filtered air system for our metal laser cutter. These improvements reduced downtime and improved air quality, contributing to a better work environment.

  • Skills: Troubleshooting, Machine Maintenance, Systems Improvement
  • Tools: CNC, Metal Laser Cutter, 3D Printers
