Lily Rassette

I’m Lily Rassette, an electrical engineering undergraduate student at Purdue University with experience in prototyping, hardware design, and system development. I’ve worked at the Bechtel Innovation Design Center, where I supported advanced prototyping projects with SLA, SLS, and FDM 3D printers, as well as laser cutters, CNC mills, and injection molding machines. I’m also currently part of the Purdue Solar Racing team, focusing on low-voltage hardware and embedded software. My long-term goal is to apply engineering to sustainability and public service projects, creating technology that drives positive environmental impact.
