Purdue Solar Racing Low Voltage Lead

As the Low Voltage Lead for Purdue Solar Racing, I design and integrate the vehicle’s low-voltage electrical systems, ensuring reliable communication and energy efficiency across subsystems. My work includes creating custom PCBs in KiCad, implementing sensor interfaces, and developing signal conditioning and power electronics for real-time data acquisition and control. I also perform extensive hardware bring-up, debugging, and validation using oscilloscopes, multimeters, and power analyzers. In addition, I collect and analyze performance data from batteries, solar cells, and power management circuits during lab and field testing to optimize the car’s resilience and efficiency over a 1500+ mile solar endurance race.
